About Us

Solargy is a rising multi-faceted company.

Solargy Group is driving the shift to clean energy operations that positively influence our planet by utilizing the most cutting-edge eco-friendly technology solutions.

We offer innovative solutions that contribute to improving your operations while attaining better environmental outcomes thanks to our diverse specialization in a wide range . We assist you in overcoming the pressing challenge of finding reliable services that employ renewable energy and sustainable solutions and sustainable solutions. We achieve this by offering innovative solutions that span numerous industries, including e-mobility, renewable energy, trading, and construction.

Solargy Group was established in 2018 and Constitutes : Solargy Renewable, which focuses on clean energy solutions; Switch with Renewable first , which specializes in electric vehicle and mobility solutions; Solargy Trading, well-known for off-shore transactions; and Solargy Construction, which provides eco-friendly construction services.
