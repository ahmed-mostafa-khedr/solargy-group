Who Are We

Inaugurated in 2018, Solargy Group overarches five companies; Solargy E-Mobility, which is specialized in electric-vehicle infrastructure and transportation solutions, Solargy Renewable that is focused on renewable energy solutions, Solargy Trading which is known for off-shore transactions and finally Solargy Construction, which specializes in eco-friendly construction services. Solargy MENA was established in order to provide the region with the expertise we have gained across of our years of operations and the training we received.

We aim to provide the MENA Region with full electric vehicle and smart parking solutions through the partnerships we have made across of the years. Specialized in diverse industries, the group offers a number of services and effective solutions that are focused on advanced technology, which shall contribute to the development and facilitation of people’s daily lives.

Our Companies

Solargy Renewable

  • Pioneering in shaping the clean energy landscape in Egypt.
  • Solargy Renewable harnesses the power of clean energy to provide smart solutions for a greener and more sustainable future. We power the transition to renewable energy in ways that better support our communities and sustain the environment. As a professional provider and integrator of solar energy systems, we plan, build, and operate utility-scale, commercial, industrial, and residential photovoltaic installations. Solargy Renewable is an EPC contractor that specializes in the design and implementation of solar power plants of all types that are uniquely designed to provide superior quality.
  • The New and Renewable Energy Authority (NREA) in Egypt has granted the company the golden certificate in recognition of the excellence that Solargy Renweble continues to provide. Solargy renewable is a registered organization in Egypt PV (EGPV) program which aims to catalyze the development of a decentralized grid-connected small-scale renewable energy generation market in Egypt.

Solargy E-mobility

  • Moving towards a more sustainable future through reliable and durable electric vehicle solutions.
  • Solargy E-mobility is a future-driven enabler and provider of electric mobility solutions that help change the way we live, transport, and impact the environment. By modernizing electric vehicle charging and parking in Egypt, we aim to facilitate the transition away from carbon emissions and toward a more sustainable future. Solargy E-mobility, founded in 2020, is a proud subsidiary of the Solargy Group.

Solargy Construction

  • Building for the future.
  • Solargy Construction is a general contractor focusing on post-tension projects for public and private clients. Our expertise enables us to execute high-quality construction projects that are environmentally conscious. Through cultivating partnerships, we are able to extend our services to serve a comprehensive portfolio of clients. By focusing on the post-tension approach, we ensure sustainability through the reduction of resources needed in construction. Therefore, offering post-tensioned designs that present both a reliable and eco-friendly solution.

Solargy Trading

  • Powering the world with solar energy.
  • Solargy Trade is a distributor of a holistic portfolio of sustainable solutions offered by Solargy group covering a wide range of brands of solar panels, inverters, EV chargers, smart parking systems, and post-tension construction services that are revolutionizing Egypt's solar power trading market. Solargy Trading serves the region’s most significant solar energy, parking solutions, and clean energy corporations in Egypt and the Middle East by being the official distributor of clean energy and by promoting optimal utilization of sustainable resources.
