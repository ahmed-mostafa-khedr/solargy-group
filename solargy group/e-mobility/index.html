About us

Moving towards a more sustainable future through reliable and durable electric vehicle solutions.

Solargy E-mobility is a future-driven electric mobility enabler and provider that helps change the way we live, transport, and impact the environment. Founded in 2020, Solargy E-mobility is a proud subsidiary of the Solargy Group. Leaving a positive environmental impact is a core value in everything we do. By modernizing electric vehicle charging and parking in Egypt, we aspire to facilitate the transition away from carbon emissions and toward a more sustainable future. We help commercial and residential clients integrate more sustainable energy solutions into their operations.

Our ultimate goal is to achieve fully sustainable transportation. We aim to lead the transformation to clean energy and accelerate MENA's adoption of e-mobility solutions by bringing cutting-edge technologies to the region, transforming it one kilometer at a time. Targeting to provide over 1000 charge points by 2025, we drive innovation and facilitate clean and sustainable mobility to the region through forefront technologies.

Rolec wallpod 22KW Residential chargers

Manufactured in the UK, the WALLPOD facelift is a low-cost charging unit, designed to offer full Mode 3 charging to every Electric Vehicle (EV/PHEV) on the market today, with the facility to charge at 3 phase at 22kW speed. This charging unit has been specifically designed for domestic locations.

Barrier Smart Parking Protections

Parklio is a smart automatic barrier that aims to troubleshoot the most persisting problem is today’s busiest and most lively areas: parking availability and regulation. From a single individual parking to hundreds of slots, parking is now accessible and worry-free, thanks to Parklio’s innovative technology and smart applications.
