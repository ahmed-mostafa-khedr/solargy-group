0315-0317「INT’L SMART GRID Expo」in Tokyo Big Sight

Here comes an exciting news!

ZEROVA TECHNOLOGIES, the electric vehicle (EV) subsidiary of Phihong Technology, is now a standalone company, and we can’t wait to demonstrate our amazing products in Japan!

Our parent company, Phihong, has already developed a family of highly efficient EV charging products, including the 50kW and 180kW DC chargers. And now, we’d love to invite you to join us at the INT’L SMART GRID Expo in Tokyo Big Sight, Japan. You can find us at Booth No. 50-6 in the East Hall, Entrance 6 from March 15th to 17th, between 10:00 AM and 6:00 PM (with the last day closing at 5:00 PM).

Come and visit us, and let’s have some fun exploring the exciting world of EV charging together!
